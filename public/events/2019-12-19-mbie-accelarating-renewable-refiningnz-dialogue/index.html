2020-04-20 Refining NZ strategic review – fuel supply implications

This briefing provides our preliminary views on the fuel supply impacts if Refining NZ were to cease its refinery operations and convert its Marsden Point facility to a fuel import terminal. Recommended action The Ministry of Business, Innovation and Employment (MBIE) recommends that you: a Note our preliminary views on the impact on fuel supply if Refining NZ were to cease refinery operations and convert is Marsden Point facilities to an import fuels terminal. Noted b Agree to write to Refining NZ and invite it to engage initially with MBIE on its strategic review.
